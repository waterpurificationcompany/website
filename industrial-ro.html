Industrial RO System


    Reverse Osmosis (RO) is a widely accepted unit operation for industrial water purification. The water is typically pressured between 150 to 600 psig and pass through either thin film composite or cellulose acetate membranes. Typical RO recoveries are between 70-90%.



The industrial RO systems is engineered as per the client's technical specifications and in accordance to the standards & requirements of the particular industry it will serve. This RO system is ideal for different types of industrial setups that required treated water. The industrial RO system is not only highly effective but also easily maintainable. These can be cleaned with ease and operated continually. These systems are cost effective and reliable.

Applications:
  • Compact and modular RO blocks
  • Easy installation, operation and servicing
  • Specific selection possible from a wide range
  • High level of plant automation
  • Visual monitoring system (VMS) for ease of trouble-shooting
  • Stainless steel multistage centrifugal pump; inline suction and delivery allows easy maintenance and access


Ion exchange treatment of the RO product water can further polish the water and make it suitable for all rinses. To design a successful and cost-effective system, WPC evaluates each individual application because the pH, oxidizing potential and concentration of soluble salts of the wastewater effluents often exceed the operating limits of the RO systems. After the detailed evaluation of the wastewater is complete, WPC determines the optimum preconditioning chemistry and selects the best pretreatment technology for the application.
