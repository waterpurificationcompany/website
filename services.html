Services


    WPC realize the importance of service and as a management team we are totally committed to providing every back up and support necessary to ensure the success of our treatment program.

WPC considers service to be a crucial part of any treatment program. Proactive professionals work flexibly, on-site and at the lab to make, test and stabilize treatment initiatives.

WPC works tirelessly to bring to the industry the latest technologically advanced products, solutions and chemistry to ensure trouble free and smooth operation of the plants.

Our Services Include:

  • System Audit/Survey.
  • Yearly “health check up” and overhauling.
  • Spares for your operating equipments.
  • Monthly Trend Reports.
  • Service chemicals and resins.
  • Treatment Review & Audit.
  • Operator Training.
  • Revamp & Augmentation needs.

Annual Maintainance Contacts
    A professional service packages which covers the Water & waste water equipments with the most trusted people from the Industry. The preventive maintenance concept is followed to deliver value to customer. Regular features of this package include scheduled inspection visits and scheduled maintenance and services. Customers can also customize this package by opting for our value added services like monitoring, Lab and Instrument support.
Operations and Maintainance Contracts
    It is a comprehensive service contract where Water Treatment Services undertakes complete responsibility of supply of treated water in required quantity and of desired quality at any client’s place. Right from Manpower to services to consumables are provided by WPC and the client is free of worries.
