About Us


    Water Purification Company was established in the year 1995 in south india at salem district, tamilnadu. We are one of the leading companies in manufacturing and supply of water treatment equipments and chemicals in india. Over two decades of plant building experience, planning, completion and operation of drinking water and waste water plants for both the commercial and industrial sectors made us an industry leader in water treatment field.

WPC offers affirm solutions for customer needs through a wide range of services and innovative technologies. Our plants facilitate environmentally compatible waste water disposal and secure access to clean drinking water for any number of people. This help us to contribute to environmental protection and enhanced quality of life.

We use our wide range of processes with an integrated approach to deliver total solutions for every market - household, institutional, commercial, industrial and public water supplies, urban and rural, in India and overseas.
