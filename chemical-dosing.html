Chemical dosing


    The dosing of water treatment chemicals needs to be precise if effective control of water treatment parameters is to be achieved. The WPC Water Treatment team have considerable experience in the selection of suitable dosing equipment. Years of water treatment experience means we can advise our customers on chemical dosing and help our customers ensure that their water treatment regime is operating as efficiently as possible.



Chemical Dosing Pumps & Controllers

    Water treatment chemicals are ideally dosed automatically via a precision chemical pump. A chemical dosing pump is controlled by equipment that will ensure the correct amount of chemical is being added for the specific application. Ensuring correct dosage through specialist equipment allows for more efficient treatments and as a result better plant protection.

Dosing pumps and controllers are commonly used in a wide verity of water treatments including:

  • Descaling treatments
  • Corrosion inhibitor treatments
  • Micro biocide treatments



Chemical Dosing Tanks

    At WPC Water Treatment our dosing tanks are specifically designed for water treatment chemicals and all have the following attributes:

  • Suitable for a wide range of chemicals
  • Graduated scale
  • Manufactured from corrosion resistant polyethylene
  • Screw cap lid
  • Split level design provides an area for mounting dosing pumps, mixers and level control
